Favourite Movies List❤️

Tourist Family

Story Line

Tourist Family follows a Sri Lankan Tamil family — Das, Vasanthi, and their two sons — who escape the economic crisis in Sri Lanka and secretly enter India for safety. They settle in Chennai by pretending to be Malayalees to avoid being treated as illegal refugees. Das struggles to earn money as a driver while the family slowly develops bonds with their neighbours, creating hope for a new beginning. But when a bomb blast happens nearby, police start targeting refugees, putting the family in danger. The film shows their emotional journey of survival, identity, fear, and the importance of humanity and support from others.

Genre: Tamil comedy-drama / family drama

Directed by: Abishan Jeevinth (his directorial debut)

Cast: Sasikumar, Simran, Mithun Jai Sankar, Kamalesh Jagan, Abhirami

Rating:8.6

Jai Bhim

Story Line

Jai Bhim follows the real-life struggle of a pregnant tribal woman, Sengeni, whose husband Rajakannu is falsely accused of theft and disappears from police custody. With no support and facing police brutality, she approaches advocate Chandru, who fights a powerful legal battle against the corrupt system. The film shows his pursuit of truth, exposing police violence and seeking justice for the oppressed Irular community.

Genre: Legal drama / Social justice drama

Directed By: T. J. Gnanavel

Cast: Suriya, Lijomol Jose, Manikandan, Prakash Raj, Rao Ramesh, Rajisha Vijayan.

Rating: 9/10
